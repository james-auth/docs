To integrate James Auth into your applications, you must:

- Have permission to integrate James Auth into your apps. You can gain consent by visiting [https://jamestheakston.pages.dev](https://jamestheakston.pages.dev) and contacting James.
- have an official privacy policy for your website if you are syncing user data to the cloud hosted on the /privacy sub-page.
- have a logo that can be replaced with a font awesome icon. The full library of FA icons are available at [https://fontawesome.com/search](https://fontawesome.com/search).
- have a site 'short name' this is the name used in the URL. e.g. if your service is called "SVG Previewer Online", your short name could be "svgonline"
