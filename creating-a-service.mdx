You can create a service by sending a request to create a James Auth developer account. You can create a developer account at [https://jamesauth.pages.dev/dev/dashboard](https://jamesauth.pages.dev/dev/dashboard). 

Once going to [https://jamesauth.pages.dev/dev/dashboard](https://jamesauth.pages.dev/dev/dashboard), you will see this screen.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot-2026-05-19-at-18.28.34.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2026 05 19 At 18 28 34"
  title="Screenshot 2026 05 19 At 18 28 34"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"71%" }}
/>

Click Sign in with James Auth, and allow access to the James Auth Beta Experiments app. 

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot-2026-05-19-at-18.30.09.png"
  alt="Screenshot 2026 05 19 At 18 30 09"
  title="Screenshot 2026 05 19 At 18 30 09"
  className="mx-auto"
  style={{ width:"66%" }}
/>

Next, fill-out the form and wait for your request to be approved.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 19 At 18 30 49](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-19-at-18.30.49.png)
</Frame>

Once you have submitted the request and it has been acknowledged, it will be put in "In review' mode.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 19 At 18 33 40](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-19-at-18.33.40.png)
</Frame>

Once it has been approved, you will have access to the dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 05 19 At 18 35 30](/images/Screenshot-2026-05-19-at-18.35.30.png)
</Frame>

Once you have access to the dashboard, please send us an email to create an application at [jamesttheakston2@gmail.com](mailto:jamesttheakston2@gmail.com) or by the form at my official website [https://jamestheakston.pages.dev](https://jamestheakston.pages.dev).
