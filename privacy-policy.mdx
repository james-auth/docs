## 1. Information We Collect

When you create a James Auth account, we store your email address and password (encrypted) to provide authentication services. If you choose to customize your profile, we store your display name and avatar URL.

## 2. How We Use Data

Your account data is used exclusively to:

- Authenticate you across verified James Theakston applications.
- Synchronize your connection status between services.
- Display your profile information within the dashboard.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use **Supabase** for database management and authentication. Your data is stored on their secure servers. We do not sell or share your personal information with any third-party advertisers or external developers.

## 4. Your Rights

You can update your profile information at any time via the James Auth settings. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact James Theakston directly.
